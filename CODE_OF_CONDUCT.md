# Code of Conduct

OpenMantis is committed to creating a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

We expect all community members to:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Refrain from harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory behavior
- Provide helpful feedback and assume good intent

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience concerning behavior, report it to the maintainers using the contact method described in `SECURITY.md` (or update this file to your preferred contact).

## Enforcement

The maintainers may remove or restrict participation at their discretion to protect community members.

If action is taken, the goal will be safety and improvement, not punishment.
